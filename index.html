Our Story



It took years for Cake Pop to be a dream come true. Ever since in our university days, we envisioned on creating our own
business in which we can share our passion to, baking. It only started as a small stand at the roadside but now we are
having our own physical store along the street of our neighborhood. We aspire to share happiness and satisfaction to
everyone by serving our best and lovingly made pastries. We look forward on having everyone in this journey.




Baked Goods



Since history was known, bread and pastries has been a staple part of our lives, it is part of our breakfast or even meriendas
partnered with almost any food (jams, hot drinks, pastas). Here at Cake Pop we only offer the very best from our ovens, we seek
perfection most of all and to satisfy everyone’s cravings and sweet tooth. We mostly specialize in cake and cupcakes, having a wide
variety of flavors and fillings.



Behind the Name



Cake Pop may sound as something weird which we also found as one, during the time we are thinking of a name, we are really stumped
but just in time the oven released a ‘pop’ sound, signaling that the cake is now cooked and ready. Since then, we decided to name it
Cake Pop as nothing is more exciting than hearing the oven timer finish and taking out a baked good out in the open, ready for everyone to share.
